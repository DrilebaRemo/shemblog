I started my journey at refactory which is tech skilling academy as well as a centre for self development and team work on 19th of February. In our first two weeks one of the modukes we got to learn was leadership and personal devt

Julia Nansubuga was our facilitator for the leadersip class She ia s great teacher and introduced us to this eye opening and life changing session

In this class, we were basically looking at how to go through maturity and grow so as to work interdependently with our co-workers.
We were guided through this journey by the 7 habits of highly effective people. A book written by the great Stephen.R.Covey

He walks us through and tells us inorder to work co-dependently with others, we must first deepen our roots so as to grow. This is by improving our character so as to imrove our personality
The 7 habits are:

  • Be proactive
  • Begin with the end in mind
  • Put important things first
  • Think win/win
  • Seek first to understand then to be understood
  • Synergy
  • Sharpen the saw
